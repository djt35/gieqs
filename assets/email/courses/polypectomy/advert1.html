Mastery of colorectal polypectomy can be difficult. With many different types and sizes of polyps technique selection is the first hurdle. An understanding of the principles of endoscopic imaging can help significantly with this.

In over 70% of colorectal polyps ≥ 20mm in size (and therefore the majority of larger polyps seen in everyday practice) [piecemeal] EMR is the technique of choice. It is safe, effective in over 90% and prevents the morbitity associated with colorectal surgery.

Want to take your next steps in colorectal polypectomy of larger polyps. We have you covered. Suitable for anyone performing or interesting in performing endoscopic mucosal resection of larger colorectal polyps.
